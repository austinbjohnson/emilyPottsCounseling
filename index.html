Hi, I’m Emily (she/her). I’m so glad you are here.

I believe healing happens when someone walks alongside you—not ahead, not behind, but beside you.

Before becoming a therapist, I spent years guiding groups through the outdoors—helping people find their footing in unfamiliar places, building trust, and creating spaces where growth could unfold. Those experiences shaped how I work today: relational, grounded, and attuned to the ways we move through the world, both inside and out.

In our sessions, humour and pain often dance together. One moment we might laugh, and the next we might explore something deeper. I create a space where all parts of you are welcome: the witty and the wounded, the confident and the confused.

Therapy, workshops, and events in Vancouver, BC.

If you’re looking for support or collaboration, you’re in the right place. My work is rooted in feminist, climate-informed, and relational care.

Therapy

I provide a confidential, supportive space for individuals and couples to explore their thoughts, feelings, and patterns.

Rooted in feminist and systems-based approaches, our work is collaborative—guided by your experiences, your insight, and your goals for healing.

Speaking

I offer speaking engagements and workshops at the intersection of climate change, mental health, and our connection to the natural world.

My talks weave together psychoeducation, reflective practices, and open dialogue to leave people feeling empowered, hopeful, and more grounded in their purpose.

Workshops

I lead nature-based workshops that support therapists in reconnecting with themselves and their work.

These gatherings focus on burnout recovery, community care, and reflection—held gently in outdoor settings.

How I work with you

What matters most isn’t the method—it’s that you feel seen, safe, and free to be yourself here.

I draw from a range of trauma-informed, relational approaches—including nature-based therapy, narrative work, and feminist theory.

These frameworks support a therapy experience that’s collaborative and deeply human. But more than anything, our work is shaped by you and your lived experience.
