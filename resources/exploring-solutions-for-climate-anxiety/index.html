Exploring Creative Solutions for Climate Anxiety - Webinar Recording

You’re not alone. In fact, studies show that 3 out of 4 Canadians feel that climate change impacts their mental health. If you are looking for resources on this topic, check out my recent talk on coping with the complex feelings associated with climate anxiety and eco-dread. Some of the key topics covered in this webinar include:

  • Understanding climate anxiety and eco-dread
  • Identifying who’s most at risk for these feelings
  • Recognizing symptoms of climate-related distress
  • Exploring mental health in a changing climate
  • Practical coping strategies for managing eco-anxiety
  • Finding joy and connection in climate action

This webinar is perfect for anyone struggling with eco-anxiety, environmental professionals, activists, or simply those looking to better understand and cope with their emotions surrounding climate change.
